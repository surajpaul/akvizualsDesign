About Me

From.a tender age I was always inclined towards sketching, colors and art.I guess the seed if a makeup artist was embedded from a very nascent stage. As I grew up I wanted to turn my hobby into a profession and since I grew up among very strong women I thought by being a trained professional I could pay a tribute to all the beautiful women out there. I believe in this saying All women are pretty but with a little bit of makeup they become pretty powerful.
History

My Experiance

I got trained in mumbai under makeup artist Michelle and thus began my journey as a makeup artist 15 years ago. As a makeup artist I do keep up with the changing trends of beauty and style and also the pantone colors of the season..
I curate looks acc to the requirements of the clients.

My strong pts are the eye makeup and base and blending coz a perfect base gives u a perfect palette. And eyes are the most expressive features and with the right amount of makeup eyes can speak a thousand words. I started my journey alone but know under me I have an accomplished team of hair stylists mehendi artists and drape artists. We also have professionals who offers a pre wedding glow package of facials and aromatics body facials.
